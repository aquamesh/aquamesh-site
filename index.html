About AquaMesh

AquaMesh designs and manufactures cutting-edge water quality monitoring systems. Our innovative technology combines high-precision sensing with seamless connectivity to provide comprehensive water quality data.

Our mission is to revolutionize water quality monitoring through advanced technology that makes data collection more efficient, accurate, and accessible than ever before.

Our Unique Technology:

  • Mesh networking between sensors using LoRa technology
  • Multiple sensing capabilities (fluorescence, absorbance, turbidity) in a single device
  • Intuitive web application for real-time monitoring and management
  • AI-powered analytics for predictive water quality insights
